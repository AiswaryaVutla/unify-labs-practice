Frequently Asked Questions

What is DevConf?
A global tech conference.
Where is it held?
Worldwide.
Are there networking sessions?
Yes, dedicated networking breaks and meetups are scheduled.
Can students attend the conference?
Yes, students are welcome and can avail discounted passes.
Is there a group registration discount?
Yes, special discounts are available for group registrations of five or more participants.
Can I edit my registration details after submitting the form?
Yes, you can update your details by logging into your registration account before the event.
What do I need to bring for entry?
Please carry a valid photo ID along with your registration confirmation email.
Is re-entry allowed during the conference?
Yes, registered attendees may exit and re-enter the venue using their badge.
Do I need to pre-register for workshops?
Yes, workshop seats are limited and require prior selection during registration.
Are beginner-friendly sessions available?
Yes, the conference includes sessions suitable for beginners, intermediates, and experts.
Will participants receive a certificate?
Yes, all attendees will receive a digital participation certificate after the event.
Can I access presentation slides after the conference?
Yes, speakers will share slides through the official conference portal.
Is the venue accessible for people with disabilities?
Yes, the venue is fully accessible and special assistance can be requested during registration.
Who can I contact during the event for support?
On-site volunteers and the help desk will be available throughout the conference.
